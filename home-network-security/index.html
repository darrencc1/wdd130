Passwords and Password Managers

In the picture to the right you can see how secure your passwords are. Remember that this changes over time and could be outdated already! This could be passwords, phone numbers, ip addresses and more. GO change your passwords! Use dedicated password manager comapanies and not your web based password managers from google or other browsers. They do not have any reason to devote tons of effort into securing your passwords, however, companies where their entire bread and butter is managing your passwords, will be much safer and way more reliable.

What to do RIGHT now!

1. Look up your email on haveibeenpwned.com 2. Get a password Manager 3. Change your passwords w. the guidlines given to the right! 4. Have a different password for everything, do NOT have the same password for your Bank account and your email!
